Introduction

Beepola is a tracker-style music editor for Windows, capable of compiling songs into a form suitable for playback on Sinclair Spectrum and compatible computers.

It aims to offer the ability to produce Spectrum music in a modern computing environment, with all of the advantages this provides.

Multiple music replay engines or "beeper engines" are supported. With the possibility of more being added in the future. Each beeper engine has its own distinctive sound and its own set of advantages and disadvantages. Once a song has been created in the Beepola editor, it can be played or compiled using any of the supported tone generators allowing the songwriter to choose the engine best suited the piece of music being created.
